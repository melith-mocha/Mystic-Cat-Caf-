EXTERIOR

The café’s exterior welcomes visitors with a warm and whimsical charm. Its soft pastel colors, cat-themed murals, and cozy outdoor seating create a friendly and relaxing vibe. Large windows let you peek inside to see the playful and peaceful atmosphere, while potted plants and gentle lighting add a natural, homey touch. It’s the perfect spot that instantly feels inviting to both pet lovers and their furry companions.
